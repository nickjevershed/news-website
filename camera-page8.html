Photofilm R800 Mirrorless Camera

The R800 is a premium mirrorless camera designed for photographers who demand high performance in a compact, stylish form. Combining a sleek, retro-inspired design with modern technology, this camera is perfect for street photography, travel, and everyday moments.

Key Features:

26.1 MP APS-C Sensor

At the heart of the R800 is a 26.1 MP X-Trans CMOS 4 sensor, which delivers incredible image quality with rich details, true-to-life colors, and excellent performance in low-light environments.

23mm f/2 Fixed Lens

The built-in 23mm f/2 lens offers sharpness and clarity, making it ideal for both wide-angle shots and stunning portraits. Its fast aperture allows for beautiful background blur and strong performance in low-light conditions.

Advanced Hybrid Viewfinder

The R800 features a hybrid viewfinder that lets you switch seamlessly between an optical viewfinder (OVF) and an electronic viewfinder (EVF), giving you the best of both worlds. This feature ensures flexibility and precision, allowing you to compose shots with greater accuracy.

4K Video Recording

Capture your moments in stunning 4K resolution. Whether you’re filming a vlog, recording a documentary, or capturing everyday moments, the R800 ensures crisp, high-quality video with beautiful color reproduction.

Weather-Sealed Design

The R800 is built to withstand the elements. Its durable, weather-sealed body ensures that you can shoot in a variety of conditions, whether you’re in the city or exploring the great outdoors.

Film Simulations

Bring a classic touch to your photos with the built-in film simulations. Choose from a variety of iconic Fujifilm film styles, including Provia, Velvia, and Acros, to give your images a unique, creative flair.

Compact and Lightweight

Weighing only 478 grams, the R800 is easy to carry and perfect for photographers on the go. Despite its small size, it offers the power and performance of a professional-grade camera, making it a versatile tool for any photography enthusiast.

Perfect for Every Occasion

Whether you’re capturing spontaneous moments on the streets, taking landscape shots during your travels, or filming a cinematic masterpiece, the R800 delivers consistent, stunning results.

Specifications:

• Sensor: 26.1 MP APS-C X-Trans CMOS 4

• Lens: 23mm f/2 fixed lens

• Video: 4K/30p recording

• Viewfinder: Hybrid OVF/EVF

• Weight: 478g (with battery and memory card)

• Weather Sealing: Yes
